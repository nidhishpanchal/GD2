Snoop Dogg's Gin and Juice

SnoopDogg is a hip-hop legend. Over the years he has expressed his love for the culinary arts and in doing so, has made a name for himself in the cooking world for his many viral recipes and business ventures. This recipe is a tribute to his 1993 hit song “Gin and Juice.”


Ingredients:

  • 2 Oz London Dry Gin
  • 2 Oz Pineapple juice
  • Club Soda
  • Pineapple Slices

Steps:

    1. In a tall glass stir the London Gin into the pineapple juice.
    2. Top with club soda to the top of the glass.
    3. Garnish with pineapple slices adn ice if desired.
