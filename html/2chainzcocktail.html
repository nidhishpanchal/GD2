2Chainz' 'Dirty Amber' Cocktail

In the last 10 or so years, 2Chainz has made a name for himself not just in the rap game, but also in the culinary world. After the release of his cookbook in 2013 titled ‘#MEALTIME’, he has continued his love for food through business ventures and entertainment. His VICELAND series titled ‘Most Expensive-ist Shit’ features some of the world's most expensive foods and services. This recipe was created by him after filming the episode where he drinks a $2,500 cocktail at a bar in Vegas. Here’s the super simple recipe;


Ingredients:

  • 2.5 oz Gin
  • 0.5 oz Lime juice
  • 1 oz Jagermeister

Steps:

    1. Combine the Gin, Jagermeister, and lime Juice in a mixing glass.
    2. Add ice and shake the contents together.
    3. Strain into a chilled glass or add additional ice after pouring.
    4. Garnish with a mint leaf if desired.
