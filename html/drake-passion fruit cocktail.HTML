Drakes Passionfruit Cocktail
  • Home
  • Drake

Drake has never hidden his love for alcohol. In fact, his Instagram handle is champagnepapi. For the last 2 or so years, He has been seen holding this drink in various pictures and videos. He even made a Day in the Life video where he showed himself making this drink. Though they may not have any relation, his 2016 album ‘More Life’ features a song titled ‘Passionfruit.’ Here's the recipe:


Ingredients:

  • 2 Oz Drake or Culverin Rum
  • .5 Oz Agave Syrup
  • 1 Oz Passionfruit Juice
  • ½ Lime
  • 5 Mint Sprigs
  • Mint Garnish

Steps:

    1. In a tall glass, muddle mint, sugar and lime juice.
    2. Stir in Agave and Passion fruit Juice and rum.
    3. Add ice and garnish if desired.
